Food for cube people...

In a vast and unknown land, a group of stoned cube beings are on a desperate search for food. They run around, their blocky bodies bouncing as they scour the terrain for any sign of sustenance. As they run, their eyes dart around, scanning the area for any possible sources of nourishment. Their fate hangs in the balance, and they knew they have to keep searching.
